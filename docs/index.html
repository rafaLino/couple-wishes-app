Wish List Todo App

A simple app to keep track of all your wife's wishes — from furniture to fun gifts — so you never lose track again!

Why I Made This

My wife often sends me links of things she wants, but I keep losing track of them in our chat. This app helps me save, organize, and manage what I've bought and what I still need to get.

Features

  • Save links of wishes quickly
  • Mark items as bought or pending
  • View all wishes in one place
  • Easily update the status of each wish

How It Works

Whenever my wife sends me a new wish, I add it to the app. I can then check off items as I buy them, making it easy to keep track of everything.

Want to Use It?

Just add your wishes, mark what you've bought, and stay organized — it's simple and fun!

Happy shopping!
